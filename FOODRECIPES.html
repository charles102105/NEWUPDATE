The first meal of the day, usually eaten in the morning, often includes eggs, toast, cereal, or fruit.

Chocolate and Banana Oats

Warm oats mixed with rich chocolate and fresh banana slices for a sweet and healthy start to your day.

SERVINGS:

  • 1-2 Servings

    • RECIPE:

  • 50 grams of oats
  • 20g vanilla (or chocolate) protein powder
  • 60ml milk of choice (I use soy)
  • 60g yoghurt
  • 1/2 banana, mashed
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1tbsp honey or maple syrup Toppings
  • chocolate chips
  • Banana slices Method

    • TIPS:

  • Mix together your oats, milk, chia seeds, mashed banana, yoghurt, maple syrup and protein powderMix together your oats, milk, chia seeds, mashed banana, yoghurt, maple syrup and protein powder
  • Leave in the fridge overnight 3. In the morning top with chocolate chips and banana slices

  • Soy sauce (toyo) - 2 tbsp

  • Sugar (asukal) - 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • All-purpose cream - 1 cup (or 250ml pack)
