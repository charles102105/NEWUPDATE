About MUGSHOT

MugShot

A franchise business committed to providing a quality serving of flavored boneless fried chicken. We also offer Chicken Shots, Shots Bucket, Half Chicken, and Whole Chicken, available in different delicious flavors such as Classic, Honey Garlic, K-Style, Spicy K-Style, Teriyaki, Cheesy Bacon, and Texas BBQ.

Treat yourself to this tasty and flavorful boneless chicken at any of our 50+ locations in the country!
